About Us

Welcome to Massé—a boutique, female-led marketing firm that believes in the power of personalized strategies and a little bit of magic. We’re not your typical, run-of-the-mill agency (yawn). We’re the grounded risk-takers, the creative rebels, and your brand’s new best friend.

Born during the pandemic out of a need to uplift small brands, Massé was founded by Anushka, a former lawyer who traded legal briefs for creative briefs. Why? Because she saw how tough it was for brands to get the spotlight they deserved—especially when they were being drowned out by cookie-cutter solutions that just didn’t cut it.

At Massé , we understand that every business has its own quirks, dreams, and challenges. We know that navigating the marketing maze can feel like running in circles with all the buzzwords, algorithms, and never-ending changes. That’s why we cut through the clutter with strategies that are just as unique as you are. No one-size-fits-all solutions here—only tailor-made strategies designed to elevate your brand and turn your audience into fans.

We’re all about those “Aha!” moments, when a creative idea clicks and makes you wonder why it hadn’t crossed your mind. We’re not afraid to color outside the lines, but rest assured, we always keep your brand’s goals in sight.

So, if you’re looking for a partner that’s just as passionate about your brand as you are, and who isn’t afraid to push the boundaries, welcome to Massé. Let’s make some noise together.

Our Services

At Massé, we offer a suite of services designed to elevate your brand, connect with your audience, and drive results. Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to refine your existing strategy, we’ve got the expertise to take your brand to the next level. Explore our core services below:

  • Brand Identity & Positioning

    Your brand is more than just a logo—it's the heart and soul of your business. We help you define and refine your brand’s identity, ensuring it stands out in a crowded market. From crafting a memorable logo to developing a cohesive brand voice and positioning, we’ll create a brand that’s not only visually appealing but also strategically aligned with your goals.

    Benefits: Build a strong, recognizable brand that resonates with your target audience and sets the stage for long-term success.

  • Digital Marketing

    In today’s digital world, visibility is everything. Our digital marketing services are designed to put your brand front and center, using data-driven strategies that drive engagement and conversions. From SEO and social media management to email marketing and PPC campaigns, we craft targeted plans that reach your audience where they are.

    Benefits: Increase your online presence, engage your audience, and turn clicks into loyal customers with customized digital marketing strategies.

  • Website Design & Development

    Your website is often the first impression customers have of your brand—let’s make it a good one. We create websites that are not only visually stunning but also user-friendly and optimized for performance. Our approach balances aesthetics with functionality, ensuring your site is a powerful tool for engaging visitors and driving action.

    Benefits: Get a custom-designed website that not only looks great but also works hard for your business, turning visitors into leads.

  • Strategy & Consulting

    Every great brand starts with a great strategy. At Massé, we dig deep to understand your business challenges and objectives, crafting strategies that are as unique as your brand. From market research and competitor analysis to growth planning and execution, our consulting services are designed to provide you with clear, actionable insights.

    Benefits: Gain clarity, direction, and a clear roadmap to achieving your business goals with strategic consulting that goes beyond the surface.

  • Creative Direction

    Creativity is at the core of everything we do. Our creative direction services bring fresh, innovative ideas to the table, helping your brand communicate in a way that’s both impactful and authentic. Whether it's a captivating campaign, a standout visual concept, or a complete brand overhaul, we ensure your brand's story is told in a compelling and cohesive manner.

    Benefits: Elevate your brand’s presence with creative direction that captures attention, sparks emotion, and drives engagement.
