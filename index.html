About Us

Welcome to Massé—a boutique, female-led marketing firm that believes in the power of personalized strategies and a little bit of magic. We’re not your typical, run-of-the-mill agency (yawn). We’re the grounded risk-takers, the creative rebels, and your brand’s new best friend.

Born during the pandemic out of a need to uplift small brands, Massé was founded by Anushka, a former lawyer who traded legal briefs for creative briefs. Why? Because she saw how tough it was for brands to get the spotlight they deserved—especially when they were being drowned out by cookie-cutter solutions that just didn’t cut it.

At Massé , we understand that every business has its own quirks, dreams, and challenges. We know that navigating the marketing maze can feel like running in circles with all the buzzwords, algorithms, and never-ending changes. That’s why we cut through the clutter with strategies that are just as unique as you are. No one-size-fits-all solutions here—only tailor-made strategies designed to elevate your brand and turn your audience into fans.

We’re all about those “Aha!” moments, when a creative idea clicks and makes you wonder why it hadn’t crossed your mind. We’re not afraid to color outside the lines, but rest assured, we always keep your brand’s goals in sight.

So, if you’re looking for a partner that’s just as passionate about your brand as you are, and who isn’t afraid to push the boundaries, welcome to Massé. Let’s make some noise together.
